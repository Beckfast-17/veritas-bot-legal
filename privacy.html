# Privacy Policy – Braxton Veritas Bot
This Privacy Policy outlines how the Braxton Veritas Bot collects and uses data.

1. Data Collected

  • Message Content – The bot may access and log messages in specific channels (e.g. #observer-suggestions) for support or automation purposes. This is only used for internal functions like logging, reminders, and content improvement.
  • User IDs & Usernames – These may be logged with suggestions for tracking and tier management. Data is never shared outside the server environment or Zapier integration.
  • Zapier Integration – If enabled, certain content (e.g. Observer suggestions) may be sent to a Google Sheet or webhook via Zapier.

2. Data Security

No sensitive data (e.g. passwords, emails, payment info) is ever collected or stored. All logs are internal and never sold or shared.

3. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the bot owner.
